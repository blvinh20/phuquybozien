## 🏝️ Overview

**Phú Quý** (formerly Phú Quý) is a beautiful island in Bình Thuận Province, known as the "Pearl of the South China Sea." The island offers pristine beaches, volcanic landscapes, and a peaceful escape from mainland Vietnam.

### Getting There
- **Cái Rè (Cà Mau) → Phú Quý:** Fast boat 1.5h (150,000-200,000 VND)
- **Vũng Tàu → Phú Quý:** Ferry 3-4h or speedboat 2h
- **Côn Đảo → Phú Quý:** 2h by speedboat

### Best Time to Visit
- **November - March:** Best weather, calm seas
- **April - October:** Monsoon season, fewer tourists but possible rough seas

## 📅 Day 1: Arrival & East Coast Exploration

### Morning (08:00 - 12:00)
- Arrive at Phú Quý port
- Check in at resort/homestay
- Light breakfast at local café

### Afternoon (14:00 - 18:00)
**Explore East Coast Beaches**
- **Bãi Đất Đen (Black Sand Beach):** Unique volcanic black sand beach
- **Mũi Đèn Lighthouse:** Climb to the iconic lighthouse for panoramic views
- Watch sunset from lighthouse area

### Evening (19:00 - 21:00)
- Dinner at beachfront restaurant (fresh seafood)
- Night walk on the beach under stars

---

## 📅 Day 2: Island Adventure

### Morning (06:00 - 11:00)
**Sunrise + Exploration**
- 05:30 Wake up for sunrise at **Hang Đọi Xẻng** or beach
- Breakfast with local specialities (bánh xèo, hải sản)
- Visit **Chùa Cầu** (Bridge Pagoda) - iconic Buddhist temple on water

### Afternoon (12:00 - 17:00)
**Beach Hopping & Snorkeling**
- **Bãi Biển Dịch (Dịch Beach):** Popular swimming spot
- **Bãi Khoái (Khoái Beach):** Secluded beach with coral reefs
- Snorkeling at coral reefs (rent gear from local operators)
- Or simply relax at the beach with a fresh coconut

### Evening (18:00 - 21:00)
**Sunset + Local Life**
- Watch sunset at **Mũi Đèn** or **Gành Đá Dựng**
- Dinner at local market (try grilled seafood, bánh tráng nướng)
- Experience local nightlife (beach bars, karaoke)

---

## 📅 Day 3: Last Moments & Departure

### Morning (07:00 - 11:00)
**Final Exploration**
- Early morning swim
- Visit **Chùa Phú Quý** (large Buddhist temple complex)
- Buy souvenirs (dried seafood, pearls, local handicrafts)
- Final breakfast

### Afternoon (12:00 - 14:00)
- Check out
- Lunch before departure
- Return to mainland

## 🍽️ Food Recommendations

### Must-Try Dishes
1. **Hải sản tươi sống** - Fresh seafood (lobster, crab, sea fish)
2. **Bánh xèo Phú Quý** - Local version of Vietnamese pancake
3. **Bánh tráng nướng** - Grilled rice paper with toppings
4. **Cháo hải sản** - Seafood porridge
5. **Dừa tươi** - Fresh coconut water
6. **Mực một nắng** - Dried squid (great souvenir)

### Restaurant Recommendations
- **Nhà hàng Hải Yến:** Fresh seafood, beachfront
- **Quán ăn Cô Ba:** Local homestyle cooking
- **Cà phê Biển:** Sunset views, coffee

## 💡 Practical Tips

### What to Bring
- Sunscreen (high SPF) -岛屿阳光强烈
- Sunglasses + hat
- Waterproof phone case
- Reef-safe sunscreen for snorkeling
- Cash (ATM limited on island)

### Transportation
- Rent motorbike: 100,000-150,000 VND/day
- Xe ôm (motorbike taxi): negotiate price
- Taxi available but limited

### Language
- Basic Vietnamese sufficient
- Some English at tourist spots

### Health
- Bring motion sickness pills for boat
- Prescription medications (limited pharmacy on island)
